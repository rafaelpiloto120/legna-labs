Delete your MyCookbook AI account

Last updated: 24 November 2025

If you no longer want to use MyCookbook AI, you can request that your account and associated data be permanently deleted. This page explains how to submit a request, what data is deleted, what may be retained, and how long the process takes.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your MyCookbook AI account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@legnalabs.com from the same email address you use to sign in to MyCookbook AI.
  2. Use the subject line: “MyCookbook AI – Delete my account”.
  3. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your full name (optional but helpful)
    • The email address associated with your MyCookbook AI account (if different from the one you are sending from)
    • A short confirmation that you want your account and data to be deleted

Once we receive your request, we will confirm by email and begin processing the deletion.

What data will be deleted

When we complete your deletion request, we will permanently delete or anonymize the following data associated with your MyCookbook AI account:

  • Your MyCookbook AI account profile and identifiers
  • Saved recipes created manually or with AI assistance
  • Imported recipes from URLs
  • Cookbooks and organizational structures you created
  • App-specific settings and preferences stored for your account

After deletion, this data will no longer be accessible in the app and cannot be recovered.

Data that may be retained

In some cases, we may need to retain limited information even after your account is deleted, for example:

  • Basic technical logs and security records required to protect our services and prevent abuse
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics that no longer identify you personally
  • Data we are legally required to keep for a certain period (for example, for accounting, fraud prevention, or compliance)

Any retained data is kept only for the purposes described above and is not used to recreate your account or rebuild your profile.

How long deletion takes

We aim to process account deletion requests as quickly as possible. In most cases:

  • You will receive an acknowledgement of your request within 7 days.
  • Your account and associated data will be deleted or anonymized within 30 days of confirming your request, except where a longer retention period is required by law.

Reinstalling or using the app again

After your account is deleted, you can still reinstall MyCookbook AI and create a new account in the future. Your previous recipes, cookbooks, and settings will not be restored.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or how your data is handled, you can contact us at:

Email: support@legnalabs.com

For more details on how MyCookbook AI uses and protects your data, please see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

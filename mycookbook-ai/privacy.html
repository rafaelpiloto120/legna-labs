Privacy Policy – MyCookbook AI

Last updated: 25/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how MyCookbook AI (“the App”), developed by Legna Labs (“we”, “us”), handles information when you use the app.

1. Data we store

MyCookbook AI allows you to create and manage content such as:

  • Recipes and cookbooks you create or import
  • Basic account information if you sign in (for example, email address)
  • Technical logs and anonymous analytics events to keep the service reliable (for example, error logs, feature usage)

2. How your data is used

We use this information to:

  • Provide the core features of the app (recipes & cookbooks)
  • Secure your account and prevent abuse
  • Understand which features are used so we can improve the app

3. Third-party services

MyCookbook AI relies on third-party services such as Firebase, storage providers, and AI APIs to deliver functionality. These services may process limited technical data (like IP addresses, device identifiers, or error logs) to keep things secure and operational.

4. Your control and choices

You can:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Request more information about your data or ask for removal, where applicable, by contacting: support@legnalabs.com

5. Children

MyCookbook AI is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@legnalabs.com

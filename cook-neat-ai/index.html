Cook N’Eat AI

Cook healthier, eat happier.

Cook N’Eat AI is your healthy and happy cooking companion, helping you turn everyday ingredients into meals, save recipes, organize cookbooks, and track what you eat in one calm place.

Why Cook N’Eat AI

Cook N’Eat AI helps you decide what to cook, save the recipes you love, and understand your day without turning food into homework.

  • Create AI recipes from ingredients you already have.
  • Import from websites and Instagram Reels, or add recipes manually.
  • Organize unlimited recipes and cookbooks with no recipe limits.
  • Track meals in My Day by text, photo, or saved recipe.
  • Review calories, protein, carbs, fat, ingredients, and quantities before saving.
  • Follow goals, trends, weight progress, preferences, and ingredients to avoid.
  • Enjoy a clean no-ads experience with optional Google sign-in and sync.

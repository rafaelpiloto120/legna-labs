# CX Radar Source Shortlist (MVP)

## Goal
Start with high-signal, low-noise sources for UCaaS/CCaaS PM monitoring and email digesting.

## Phase 1 (first 2 weeks)
Use these as default watchlist:

1. Industry media
- CX Today: https://www.cxtoday.com/
- UC Today: https://www.uctoday.com/

2. Core competitors (your list)
- Talkdesk news/press: https://www.talkdesk.com/news-and-press/press-releases/
- Five9 news releases: https://five9.gcs-web.com/news-releases
- Genesys newsroom announcements: https://www.genesys.com/company/newsroom/announcements
- RingCentral press releases: https://www.ringcentral.com/whyringcentral/company/pressreleases-2025.html

3. Additional high-relevance competitors/platforms
- NICE news: https://www.nice.com/company/news
- Zoom newsroom (Contact Center): https://news.zoom.com/
- Amazon Connect updates: https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/
- Twilio press releases: https://www.twilio.com/en-us/press/releases

4. Regulatory and policy
- FCC AI robocall ruling docs: https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-400393A1.pdf
- FCC declaratory ruling text: https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/FCC-24-17A1_Rcd.pdf
- FTC impersonation rule guidance: https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/blog/2024/02/new-impersonator-rule-gives-ftc-powerful-tool-protecting-consumers-businesses
- EU AI Act implementation timeline: https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/regulatory-framework-ai

## Why these sources
- They combine vendor-first announcements, independent media coverage, and policy/regulatory signals.
- They cover both product moves (AI agents, pricing, bundles, integrations) and risk/compliance shifts.
- They map directly to PM decisions: roadmap, packaging, positioning, and launch risk management.

## Phase 2 tuning (after 2 weeks)
1. Keep only sources that generated at least 2 useful insights.
2. Remove sources with repeated low-value or duplicated stories.
3. Add segment-specific sources (healthcare, fintech, retail CX) based on your ICP.

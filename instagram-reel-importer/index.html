Turn a public Instagram Reel into a recipe draft

This project gives people a fast way to try one of MyCookbook AI’s most useful capabilities: turning cooking inspiration from Instagram into a structured recipe draft you can review, refine, and take into the app.

Only public Reel links Drafts should be reviewed before cooking Made to lead into MyCookbook AI

Only public Instagram Reel links are supported.

Paste a Reel URL to generate a draft recipe.

Daily limit: 5 recipe drafts per visitor.

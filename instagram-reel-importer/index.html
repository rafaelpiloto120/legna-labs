Turn a public Instagram Reel into a recipe draft

This project gives people a fast way to try one of Cook N’Eat AI’s most useful capabilities: turning cooking inspiration from Instagram into a structured recipe draft you can review, refine, and take into your healthy cooking routine.

Only public Reel links Drafts should be reviewed before cooking Made to lead into Cook N’Eat AI

Only public Instagram Reel links are supported.

Paste a Reel URL to generate a draft recipe.

Daily limit: 5 recipe drafts per visitor.
